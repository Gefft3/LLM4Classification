      <div class="field field-name-title field-type-ds field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even" property="dc:title"><h1>Mpox Cases Significantly Increase in the USA</h1></div></div></div><div class="subtitle">December 28, 2023 • 3:53 pm CST</div><div class="field field-name-field-image field-type-image field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even" rel="" resource="https://www.vax-before-travel.com/sites/default/files/styles/article_metadata/public/Screenshot%202023-12-28%203.39.37%20PM.png"><img typeof="foaf:Image" class="img-responsive" src="https://www.vax-before-travel.com/sites/default/files/styles/article_metadata/public/Screenshot%202023-12-28%203.39.37%20PM.png" width="1200" height="800" alt=""></div></div></div><div class="field field-name-field-image-caption field-type-text field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even" property="">US CDC mpox case map Dec. 2023</div></div></div><div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded"><span class="pretext-geo"> (Vax-Before-Travel)</span><p>The World Health Organization (WHO) recently published its External Situation Report #31 regarding the ongoing, multi-country outbreak of mpox.</p>
<p>Based on the data <a href="https://www.who.int/publications/m/item/multi-country-outbreak-of-mpox--external-situation-report-31---22-december-2023">reported</a> on December 22, 2023, the mpox outbreak continues in most WHO regions,&nbsp;while more extensive transmission has been&nbsp;observed in the European Region and the Region of the Americas.</p>
<p>A total of 906 additional mpox&nbsp;cases were reported in November 2023,&nbsp;representing a 26% increase compared to&nbsp;October.</p>
<p>The United States of America (299 vs. 135) reported the most significant&nbsp;increase in the Americas. The states of California, New York, and Texas have reported the most mpox cases.</p>
<p>Around half (52%) of cases with available information in this outbreak are reported to&nbsp;be in persons living with HIV.&nbsp;</p>
<p>The U.S. CDC's Advisory Committee on Immunization Practices&nbsp;voted on October 25, 2023, to recommend the routine use of the <a href="https://www.precisionvaccinations.com/vaccines/jynneos-mva-bn-smallpox-mpox-vaccine">JYNNEOS®&nbsp;vaccine</a>&nbsp;for people&nbsp;at risk of mpox infection.</p>
<p>However, only 23% of the at-risk population has been&nbsp;fully vaccinated&nbsp;nationally as of December 2023.</p>
<p>On December 7, 2023, the CDC published an advisory stating the JYNNEOS is&nbsp;expected to be effective for both Clade I and Clade II MPXV infections. However, real-world data is lacking, reported the&nbsp;European CDC.</p>
<p>According to the CDC,&nbsp;approximately 1.2 million JYNNEOS&nbsp;vaccine&nbsp;doses have been administered in the U.S. since the mpox outbreak began in May 2022.</p>

Ministry of Health Palitha Mahipala told a press conference that moreover, children aged nine months to 15 years who have not received the MMR vaccine, can get vaccinated from Jan. 6 next year.</p><p>   There have been over 700 cases of measles reported nationwide since May this year, mostly in the Colombo district, he said.</p><p>   Cases have also been reported from the Gampaha, Kalutara, Galle, Matara, Kandy, Jaffna, Kurunegala, and Ampara districts.</p><p>   The health ministry will establish 1,600 clinics across the South Asian country for people to access the vaccine, according to Mahipala.</p><p>   In 2019, the World Health Organization (WHO) said that Sri Lanka had successfully eradicated the contagious disease thanks to a successful vaccination campaign.</p><p>   Mahipala

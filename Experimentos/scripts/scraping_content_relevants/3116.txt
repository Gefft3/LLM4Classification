      <div class="field field-name-title field-type-ds field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even" property="dc:title"><h1>Chikungunya Vaccine Candidate Completes BLA Submission to U.S. FDA</h1></div></div></div><div class="subtitle">December 23, 2022 • 1:44 am CST</div><div class="field field-name-field-image field-type-image field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even" rel="" resource="https://www.vax-before-travel.com/sites/default/files/styles/article_metadata/public/application-3695107%20%281%29.jpg"><img typeof="foaf:Image" class="img-responsive" src="https://www.vax-before-travel.com/sites/default/files/styles/article_metadata/public/application-3695107%20%281%29.jpg" width="1200" height="800" alt=""></div></div></div><div class="field field-name-field-image-caption field-type-text field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even" property="">by Adrian P.</div></div></div><div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded"><span class="pretext-geo"> (Vax-Before-Travel)</span><p>An innovative vaccine candidate for preventing chikungunya infections is fast approaching authorization in the U.S. The&nbsp;medical need for this vaccine is clear, as millions of chikungunya cases&nbsp;have been reported in the Americas and over&nbsp;100 countries.</p>
<p>Furthermore, this mosquito-borne viral disease's related economic impact is considered&nbsp;significant.</p>
<p>France-based specialty vaccine company Valneva SE&nbsp;announced&nbsp;today that it had completed the rolling submission of the Biologics License Application (BLA) to the U.S. Food and Drug Administration (FDA) for its single-shot chikungunya vaccine candidate, <a href="https://www.precisionvaccinations.com/vaccines/valneva-chikungunya-vaccine-vla1553">VLA1553</a>.</p>
<p>Valneva is seeking approval from the FDA for VLA1553&nbsp;in adults&nbsp;aged 18 years and above.</p>
<p>The FDA will now review the filing for acceptance, determine priority review eligibility, and the action date that it targets to complete its evaluation.</p>
<p>This VLA1553 vaccine development&nbsp;program previously received FDA Fast Track and Breakthrough Therapy designations.</p>
<p>Juan Carlos Jaramillo, MD, Chief Medical Officer of Valneva, commented in a press release on December 23, 2022, "The completion of our BLA submission is extremely important as it takes us a step closer to potentially bringing a preventative solution to fight this debilitating disease."</p>
<p>"Chikungunya is a significant public health threat transmitted to humans by infected mosquitoes, and no vaccine or specific treatments for the disease are currently available."</p>
<p>"If the FDA approves the submission, our goal is to provide a tool to help curtail this growing, unmet medical need."</p>
<p>This BLA application follows the final pivotal Phase 3 data reported in March 2022 and the final lot-to-lot consistency results reported in May 2022.</p>
<p>A clinical study of VLA1553 in adolescents is ongoing in Brazil, which may support future regulatory submissions in this group if VLA1553 is initially approved in adults.</p>
<p>The Company also recently reported positive antibody persistence data with a 99% seroresponse rate 12 months after a single-dose vaccination.</p>
<p>VLA1553 was also granted PRIority MEdicine&nbsp;designation by the European Medicines Agency&nbsp;in 2020, and Valneva plans to make regulatory submissions for VLA1553 in Europe in the second half of 2023.</p>
<p>Chikungunya is a mosquito-borne viral disease caused by the chikungunya virus (CHIKV), a Togaviridae virus transmitted by Aedes mosquitoes.</p>
<p>Infections lead&nbsp;to symptomatic disease in 72-92% of humans after four to seven days following the mosquito bite.</p>
<p>While mortality with CHIKV is low, morbidity is high.</p>
<p>Clinical symptoms include acute onset of fever, debilitating joint and muscle pain, headache, nausea, rash, and chronic arthralgia.</p>
<p>CHIKV&nbsp;often causes sudden large outbreaks with high attack rates, affecting one-third to three-quarters of the population in areas where the virus is circulating.</p>
<p>As of December 23, 2022, there are no CHIKV preventive vaccines or effective treatments available, and, as such, chikungunya is considered a major public health threat.</p>
<p>If approved, Valneva intends to commercialize VLA1553, leveraging its existing manufacturing and commercial operations.</p>
<p>Other&nbsp;chikungunya vaccine development news is posted at <a href="https://www.precisionvaccinations.com/chikungunya-vaccines">PrecisionVaccinations.com</a>.</p>
